Publications

I am a CS graduate student at the University of South Florida where I work as a research assistant at the Reality, Autonomy, and Robot Experience (RARE) Lab directed by Professor Zhao Han. I hold a bachelor’s degree in mechanical engineering from the Federal University of Technology Akure, Nigeria (FUTA). My research interests lie in Robotics, Human-Robot Interaction (HRI), and Artificial Intelligence (AI).

I am currently working on developing an augmented reality (AR) technology called fog screen, which allows in-the-air projection for robots to communicate with humans in environments that lack projectable surfaces.
